How it works

For testing purposes, we have currently only included naive baselines in CloudCV VQA

  1. You upload an image.
  2. Your request is sent to our servers with GPUs courtesy NVIDIA.
  3. Our servers run a simple VQA algorithm.
  4. Results and updates are shown in real-time.
